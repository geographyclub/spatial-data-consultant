Websites

Publishing geographic and scientific data on the web. Specializing in single-purpose data-driven websites for academic or commercial research.


American Geography

Quick yet revealing look at how States rank in every census category. Processing and serving the latest American Community Survey.


World Factbook Scraper

A seemingly endless feed of country facts—the geographer’s bathroom reader. Retrieves and parses data on-the-fly from CIA World Factbook JSON files, with over 250 overview maps.


ASEAN Primer

An economic summary of ASEAN nations compiled from the latest official data, with a colorful ranking system and regional map.

Resources

Weather & Climate

Global forecasts of potential weather scenarios up to 16 days at 10 to 15km resolution using the Canadian GEM model.


Current weather reports of wind, sky condition, temperature, dew point and more at over 5,000 METAR stations around the world.


Long-term trends in temperature and precipitation thru the Global Historical Climatology Network and WorldClim datasets.


Remote Sensing

Global 30m Digital Terrain Model from the Japanese Aerospace Exploration Agency's ALOS World 3D -30m.


Global 90m and 30m Digital Elevation Model from NASA's Shuttle Radar Topography Mission SRTM.


Medium-resolution multispectral imagery from NASA's workhorse Landsat program for land cover, forest management, biodiversity and more.


Biodiversity

Global compendium of hydrological and environmental characteristics for sub-basins, rivers and lakes at multiple scales from HydroATLAS.


The WWF's Terrestrial Ecoregions of the World provides a map of 867 terrestrial ecoregions, classified into 14 different biomes such as forests, grasslands or deserts for conservation and planning.


The Global Biodiversity Information Facility GBIF is an international network funded by the world's governments and aimed at providing open access to data about all types of life on Earth.


Planning & Development

OpenStreetMap is a community-driven map of the world—the open source Google Maps—with extensive data on roads, buildings, places and more.


Maps of population, demographics and dynamics from Global Human Settlement Layer and a focus on low and middle income countries from WorldPop.


The premier source for population and housing information on the US from the US Census American Community Survey.


Wikidata is the central storage for structured data of projects including Wikipedia, Wikivoyage, Wiktionary, Wikisource and others.


The World Factbook provides basic intelligence on the history, people, government, economy, energy, geography, environment, communications, transportation, military, terrorism and transnational issues for 266 world entities.
