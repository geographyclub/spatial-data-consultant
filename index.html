Spatial Analysis

Over 15 years of experience in spatial data processing, databasing, visualization and analysis. Areas of interest:


Weather

Working with global forecasts of potential weather scenarios from the Canadian GEM model. Also, current weather reports of temperature, sky condition, humidity and wind from METAR data.


Remote Sensing

Doing terrain and hydrological analysis on global 30m and 90m digital elevation models from ALOS and SRTM. Also, image classification and vegetation indexing on medium-resolution multispectral imagery from Landsat and MODIS.


Planning & Development

Working with OpenStreetMap geographic and tag data to explore spatial relationships in the city. Like looking at amenities, infrastructure and other street-level data against population and housing data from the US Census.
